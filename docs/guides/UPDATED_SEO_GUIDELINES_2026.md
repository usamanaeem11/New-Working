## üéØ MODERN SEO SPECIFICATIONS

### Meta Data Requirements

#### Meta Title (50-60 characters)
- **Length:** 50-60 characters (strict)
- **Structure:** [Power Word] [Focused Keyword] [%currentyear%]
- **Power Words:** Ultimate, Complete, Best, Top, Essential, Proven
- **Example:** "Best Time Tracking Software %currentyear%"
- **Character Count:** Must include spaces

#### Meta Description (150-160 characters)
- **Length:** 150-160 characters (strict)
- **Must Include:**
  - Focused keyword (first 100 characters)
  - Benefit statement
  - Call-to-action
  - %currentyear% variable
- **Example:** "Discover the best time tracking software for %currentyear%. Boost productivity by 40%, eliminate time theft, and automate payroll. Start free trial today!"

#### URL Slug (3-4 words max)
- **Format:** /keyword-keyword-keyword/
- **Max Words:** 3-4
- **Example:** /time-tracking-software/
- **No:** years, articles (a, the), conjunctions
